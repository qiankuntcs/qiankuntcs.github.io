Short Bio

I am now an associate professor (research) of Cyber Science & Engineering at Huazhong University of Science & Technology . I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Hong Kong under Zhiyi Huang in 2021, where I was a recipient of the Postgraduate Fellowships.

Before that I got a bachelor degree from the Chu Kochen Honors College under Guochuan Zhang at Zhejiang University in 2017, where I was awarded the Dean's Scholarship. I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science, particularly in the design and analysis of online algorithms.

My previous research achieved the state-of-the-art results for several significant generalizations of the classical online bipartitle matching problem.
